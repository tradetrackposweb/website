Two Terminals. One System.

Run two counters simultaneously without stepping on each other's toes

TradeTrack POS runs two fully independent POS terminals (POS 1 and POS 2) in parallel. Each has its own cart, its own customer, and its own payment flow. One cashier serves a walk-in while another processes a workshop's account order — simultaneously.

  • Search by part name, OEM number, barcode or cross-reference in milliseconds
  • Image preview on hover — confirm the right part before adding to cart
  • Loyalty points automatically earned and shown at checkout
  • Lay-by with configurable minimum deposit and instalment tracking
  • Quotes that can be recalled and converted to invoices
  • Biometric fingerprint login — no PINs to forget or share
  • Lost sale recording — know exactly what you didn't have in stock
  • Online orders processing and catalog browsing mode

Security & Reliability

Enterprise-grade security,
zero cloud risk

Your data stays on your machine. TradeTrack runs locally — not in someone else's cloud. Backups go to your OneDrive. Every action leaves an audit trail.

👆

Biometric Fingerprint Login

Staff log in with their fingerprint. No PINs to share or forget. Each transaction is tied to a verified identity. Fast, secure, and audit-ready.

🔐

Role-Based Access Control

Five permission levels: Admin, Manager, Supervisor, Cashier, Driver. Every module and feature is individually togglable per role. Cashiers can't see costs. Drivers only see their jobs.

☁️

Encrypted Cloud Backups

Automatic daily backups to your own OneDrive — encrypted, compressed, and scheduled. Keep 30 days of history. Email notification on completion or failure.

📝

Full Authorization Audit Trail

Every manager override, price markdown, and supervisor approval is logged with employee, timestamp and reason. The Authorization Log report gives you a complete picture.

💻

Local-First Architecture

TradeTrack runs as a desktop app on your hardware. Your data never leaves your premises. No monthly subscription to a cloud service. Works without internet.

Automatic Updates

One-click updates delivered silently in the background. New features and fixes arrive automatically. Schedule updates for after hours so your store is never disrupted.
